<h2>2. Purposes of Processing</h2>

<ul>

<li>Saving your progress and settings locally on the device.</li>

<li>Aggregated statistics to balance levels, fix bugs and improve gameplay.</li>

<li>Install attribution and marketing performance analysis (AppsFlyer).</li>

</ul>


<h2>3. Data Sharing</h2>

<p>No personal data is sold or transferred to third parties.  

Aggregated analytics is processed only by the following providers under their own privacy policies:</p>

<ul>

<li>Unity Analytics — <a href="https://unity.com/legal/game-player-and-app-user-privacy-policy">unity.com/legal/game‑player‑and‑app‑user‑privacy‑policy</a></li>

<li>GameAnalytics — <a href="https://gameanalytics.com/privacy">gameanalytics.com/privacy</a></li>

<li>AppsFlyer — <a href="https://www.appsflyer.com/legal/services-privacy-policy/">appsflyer.com/legal/services‑privacy‑policy</a></li>

</ul>


<h2>4. Retention and Deletion</h2>

<p>

Local data remain until you delete the app or clear its data.  

Analytics data are retained by the above providers for the period necessary to provide their services.  

</p>


<h2>5. Your Rights</h2>

<p>

You have the right to request access to, correction, or deletion of your data at any time. 

Removing the app deletes all local data immediately.

</p>


<h2>6. Data Deletion Requests</h2>

<p>

To request deletion of your analytics data, please send an email to <a href="mailto:starminersteam@gmail.com">starminersteam@gmail.com</a> with subject "Data Deletion Request". 

Include your device ID if possible (though this is not required).

We will process your request and forward it to the relevant analytics providers within 30 days.

</p>
